About Me

I am a navy veteran. I started my career in the IT world in the Navy as an Electronic Technician working on Satelite Communications. When I left the Navy I worked as a Help Desk Technician for an Electronic Medical Record company for 4 years. I moved from this company to the current place of employment in 2011 as a Test Engineer. I have been in my current role for 11.5 years. My current title is Test Engineer Specialist. During my time I have received a Bachelors in Information Technology wiht a specialization in Software Development.
